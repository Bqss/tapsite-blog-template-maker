Puppy Training Quick Tips

Essential tips for successful puppy training

🕐

Start Early

Begin training as soon as your puppy arrives home, ideally between 8-16 weeks old.

🎯

Be Consistent

Use the same commands and rewards every time to avoid confusing your puppy.

🏆

Positive Rewards

Always reward good behavior immediately with treats, praise, or play.

⏰

Short Sessions

Keep training sessions to 5-10 minutes for optimal attention and learning.

👥

Socialize Early

Expose your puppy to different people, animals, and environments safely.

🧘

Stay Patient

Every puppy learns at their own pace. Patience leads to lasting success.
