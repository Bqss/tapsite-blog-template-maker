About Me

I'm a developer who believes that great technology should be invisible, intuitive, and genuinely helpful to the people who use it.

With over 8 years of experience in full-stack development, I've had the privilege of working with startups and Fortune 500 companies, helping them bring their digital visions to life. My journey began with a simple HTML page and has evolved into architecting complex systems that serve millions of users.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me writing about technology trends, mentoring junior developers, or exploring the great outdoors with my camera. I believe the best solutions come from understanding both the technical challenges and the human stories behind them.
